Our Story

  • TICA Registered Breeder
  • Genetic health testing (HCM, SMA, PKDef)
  • Raised in family environment
  • International delivery experience

Our European Maine Coon breeding program is something we are very proud of at Wind in Willows. As a TICA-registered cattery established in 2009, we are dedicated to raising healthy, happy cats that reflect strong breed standards. Our cats have also earned champion titles at many cat exhibitions across Europe.

We believe every kitten deserves a loving home, and we work carefully to place our Maine Coon kittens with the best families possible. From an early age, kittens are handled and socialized daily so they grow into confident, well-balanced, and affectionate companions.
