Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our Maine Coons

What makes Wind in Willows Maine Coons special?

We specialize in rare traits that are difficult to find: blue-eyed Maine Coons, polydactyl (extra-toed) Maine Coons, and stunning silver coat varieties. Our bloodlines carry TICA show champion genetics and have been refined over 15+ years of dedicated breeding.

Do you ship Maine Coon kittens internationally?

Yes — our kittens have traveled to families across the USA, Canada, Europe, and Asia. We handle all the necessary documentation, health certificates, and logistics to make international shipping as smooth as possible.

Are your kittens health tested and vaccinated?

Absolutely. All our breeding cats undergo comprehensive genetic testing including HCM screening and DNA panels for hereditary conditions. Every kitten leaves our cattery fully vaccinated, microchipped, and with a complete health record and pedigree certificate.

How do I reserve a Maine Coon kitten?

Browse our available kittens, then reach out via our contact page. We'll discuss the kitten's details, answer your questions, and guide you through the reservation and purchase process.

What is included when I purchase a kitten?

Each kitten comes with full TICA pedigree documentation, vaccination records, genetic test results, a signed purchase contract with health guarantees, breeder support for the lifetime of your cat, and a kitten care starter pack with food and advice.
