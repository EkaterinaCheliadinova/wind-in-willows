Kitten Adoption Policies

We are dedicated to ensuring that our Maine Coon kittens go to loving homes and that the adoption process is transparent and smooth. Please read our policies below.

1. Age and Readiness

Our kittens are ready to leave for their new homes at 4 months of age. By this time, they will have:

  • Completed a thorough health check by a veterinarian.
  • Been vaccinated, microchipped, and dewormed.
  • Been socialized to adapt to their new environments comfortably.

2. Health and Parent Testing

If you are purchasing for breeding purposes, genetic tests can be requested for an additional fee of $300. All parent or grandparent cats undergo testing for HCM, PK def, and SMA. Since we exclusively employ genetically healthy cats for breeding, testing for every litter is unnecessary. Kittens are guaranteed to be free from genetic diseases.

3. Kittens Sold as Pets

For kittens purchased as pets, they will be neutered before leaving our cattery. This ensures responsible pet ownership.

4. TICA Pedigree

All kittens come with an official TICA pedigree, certifying their lineage and breed quality.

5. Contract of Purchase

We provide an official purchase contract for every adoption. This guarantees the safety and well-being of the kitten during transit.

6. Reservation Policy

To reserve a kitten, we require:

  • A 30% deposit for kittens sold as pets.
  • A 50% deposit for kittens sold for breeding purposes.
