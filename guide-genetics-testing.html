Guide: Genetics & Testing

Testing policy should be transparent and document-based. Always ask for report copies, not only verbal confirmation.

Core Tests to Verify

ConditionWhy it mattersWhat to request
HCM (MYBPC3)Hereditary cardiac riskParent DNA status + cardiology monitoring approach
SMANeuromuscular diseaseDNA status of both parents + compatible pairing
PKDefBlood enzyme deficiency riskParent DNA status and litter risk explanation
Blood typeImportant for breeding safety planningCattery policy for blood typing breeding cats

Document Checklist Before Reservation

  • Named sire/dam test reports or official lab summaries.
  • Clear explanation of how parent pairing avoids affected kittens.
  • Written health guarantee terms in the contract.
  • Vaccination and vet records provided before pickup/shipping.

Breeding for type/color never replaces health-first selection. Ask for both.
