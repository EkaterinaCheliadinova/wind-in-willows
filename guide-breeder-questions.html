Guide: Questions for Breeders

These questions help you compare breeders objectively and avoid avoidable risk.

Top Questions to Ask

  1. Which genetic tests were completed on sire and dam, and can you share report copies?
  2. What vaccinations and preventive treatments are already completed for this kitten?
  3. What is covered in the purchase contract and health guarantee?
  4. How are kittens socialized before leaving the cattery?
  5. How do you evaluate temperament and match kittens to families?
  6. What post-adoption support is available after arrival?
  7. What feeding, grooming, and transition routine do you recommend for week one?

Good Signs in Answers

  • Clear, specific, document-based responses.
  • No pressure to reserve before you review records.
  • Honest discussion of strengths and limits of each kitten.
  • Willingness to support you after adoption, not only before payment.

If you want, we can help you compare available kittens based on color goals, temperament, and family lifestyle.
