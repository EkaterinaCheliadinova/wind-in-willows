About Us

Our Story

Wind in Willows Maine Coon Cattery was founded in Moscow, Russia in 2009 by Elena Cheliadinova and her family. What started as a profound love for one of the world's most majestic cat breeds has grown into one of the most recognized TICA-registered Maine Coon catteries in Russia — with kittens now living in homes across the USA, Canada, Europe, and Asia.

We specialize in traits that are rare even among Maine Coons: blue-eyed Maine Coons, polydactyl Maine Coons (extra-toed cats with remarkable paws), and breathtaking silver and smoke coat varieties. Every breeding decision is made with the goal of preserving these extraordinary characteristics while improving health, structure, and temperament.

Health is non-negotiable at Wind in Willows. All our breeding cats undergo comprehensive genetic testing — including HCM cardiac screening and DNA panels for hereditary conditions — so that every kitten we place carries the best possible foundation for a long, healthy life. Kittens leave our care fully vaccinated, microchipped, and accompanied by their TICA pedigree and complete health documentation.

Our dedication to quality has earned us recognition at TICA shows at home and abroad. But our greatest reward is the messages we receive from families around the world — their Wind in Willows Maine Coon has become the heart of their home.
