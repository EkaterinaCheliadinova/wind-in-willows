About Us

Our Story

Our European Maine Coon breeding program is something we are very proud of at Wind in Willows. As a TICA-registered cattery established in 2009, we are dedicated to raising healthy, happy cats that reflect strong breed standards. Our breeding cats are selected for excellent genetics, verified health, and friendly, balanced personalities. Our cats have also earned champion titles at many cat exhibitions across Europe.

We believe every kitten deserves a loving home, and we work carefully to place our Maine Coon kittens with the best families possible. From an early age, kittens are handled and socialized daily so they grow into confident, well-balanced, and affectionate companions.

Health standards guide every step of our program. Kittens leave our care fully vaccinated, microchipped, and accompanied by complete TICA pedigree and health documentation, so families can welcome their new companion with confidence.

We are grateful to stay connected with families long after adoption and to see Wind in Willows kittens become treasured members of homes across the world.
